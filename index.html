Taniti Island

Current Conditions: 29°C / 84°F ☀️

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific with an area of less than 500 square miles. The terrain includes sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, and a mountainous interior with an active volcano.

The island has an indigenous population of about 20,000. While historically focused on fishing and agriculture, tourism is now a major part of the economy.
